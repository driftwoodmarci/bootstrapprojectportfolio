ABOUT US

Founded in 2012 by a group of artists, freelancers, and entrepreneurs, Nest Cowork is a bright, inviting space offering open floor plans as well as private offices and spacious meeting spaces. Our facility features comfortable, ergonomic furniture, a fully-stocked coffee and water bar with espresso machine, and mail room with courier services. Located in the historic district of downtown Tacoma, you’ll be surrounded by the artistic and entrepreneurial spirit that makes Nest Cowork shine.
